Vote Smarter, Vote Faster Welcome to VoteEase

A Fast, Secure, and Hassle-Free Way to Cast your Vote!

An online voting system for Emilio Aguinaldo College-Cavite that will be used for an efficient management of school voting events.

This new system will replace the old system of voting in EAC-Cavite and create a simple platform for a faster, mass voting with minimal errors and inefficiencies of the current method.

This eliminates manual tabulations from process, and in doing so brings accuracy and reliability to elections, saving more time and money and enabling increased participation.

About Us

VoteEase is a fast, secure, and hassle-free digital voting system designed to streamline elections and decision-making at Emilio Aguinaldo College-Cavite.

Our Mission

To provide a fast, secure, and user-friendly digital voting system that ensures fair, accurate, and efficient elections for the Emilio Aguinaldo College-Cavite community.
